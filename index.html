HTML stands for hypertext markup language.
It is the heart of the web and made up of text content, markup, reference to documents, and links to other pages. The web consist of someone using a computer with a web brower and over the internet connects to a server. It then pulls the information from the server and displays on the computer. In between the internet and servers you will find http.

Inline vs. Block
Inline is more focused on the break and includes a, span, strong, img, br.
Block is a parapgraph with invisible boxes around the text. This includes div, form, and p.
